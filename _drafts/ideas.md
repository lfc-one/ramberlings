# Ideas

In depth look at invokeSpecial invokeStatic, invokeInterface, invokeVirtual and invokeDynamic.  The idea is to have a step by step description and ability to show how 
these work.

References:
https://blogs.oracle.com/javamagazine/post/understanding-java-method-invocation-with-invokedynamic
https://blogs.oracle.com/javamagazine/post/mastering-the-mechanics-of-java-method-invocation - this has a good starting point, but I want to go into simplier more depth 
on how the class is structured etc in java.

A Follow on from this would be looking at how the JIT then changes this.
